Cyberminer - Search

Welcome to the search page!

There are three types of searches you can perform:
Basic (OR) search: search for all of the given terms
Format: enter the search terms separated by spaces (e.g. "social networking")
AND search: search for terms that contain both the first and second word
Format: enter the search words separated by the word AND and spaces (e.g. "social AND networking")
Note: there should be no more than two word this way: the one before AND and the one after
NOT search: search for the links that contain the first word but not the second word
Format: enter the search words separated by the word NOT and spaces (e.g. "social NOT networking")
Note: there should be no more than two word this way: the one before NOT and the one after
